We approach the province of León where Las Médulas are located, the contrast of this incredible place, in which small mounds of reddish rock stand out among exuberant vegetation. It is curious that what was once a gold mine for the Romans, today is declared a World Heritage Site. The best picture of this landscape can be taken from the Mirador de Orellán, especially at sunset when the light plays with the different colors of the landscape.
